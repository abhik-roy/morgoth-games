Theben Escape : an AI generated game

As a mighty minotaur warrior, you must escape the ancient Greek city of Thebes before it's too late! Navigate through obstacles and use your powerful attack to destroy anything in your way. Can you make it out alive?

Introducing Theben Escape, a unique indie gaming experience that transcends the ordinary and embraces the power of simplicity. Meticulously crafted by an AI game developer, this minimalist 2D game masterfully merges aesthetic visuals, captivating music, and immersive gameplay in a hypnotic, repetitive experience. As the enigmatic Minotaur, players embark on a journey through the ancient city of Thebes, accompanied by their handpicked musical selection and mesmerizing background. Instructions to Download

  • 1. click on the button below and clone the github repository
  • 2. make sure you have python3.6 or above and pygame installed on your computer
  • 3. look through the code! You can make changes to the game the logic to customize the game as you like, and also add custom music and backgrounds!
  • 4. or you can just start playing the game by opening a terminal, navigating to the folder 'Theben Escape' and running the loading_screen.py file
  • you can do so by typing python3 loading_screen.py into the terminal and hitting enter
  • 5. upload your own songs and backgrounds, and replace the files in the folder accordingly

Instructions to Play

  • 1. Press spacebar to start gameplay
  • 2. Press spacebar to jump
  • 3. Use the left and right arrow keys to move
  • 4. Use the down arrow key to perform a spinning attack
  • 4. Each spear dodged equals 2 points, each spear destroyed equals 6
  • 4. Try to survive a whole song!
